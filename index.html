Find a greener way!

A travel app to enable people to make a more environmentally informed decision when planning a holiday. It provides users with estimated CO2e emissions for different destinations and vehicle types as well as suggesting local alternatives.

Never miss a beat!

Endless music festival lineups can make it a bit daunting choosing where to be and when, knowing that you may be missing some of your favourite acts or running back and forth and not seeing any! This is where frontleft comes in; a planner to help you enjoy every moment.

Share your World!

A social media platform built during a two week group project at Makers. This was my first introduction to the MERN stack. We were given an existing codebase to initially understand and then to add features to.

Shop 'till you drop!

A till app that renders a menu of your making into an interactive blackboard. Take the name for the order and add items of their choosing before submitting the order and printing your receipt! This started as a mock tech test, before I chose to build it up into a small app.

Welcome to the superbowl!

A bowling scorecard calculator to take the (surprisingly complex) bowling maths out of your hands. This was an exercise in planning and TDD due to the tricky logic underpinning a bowling scorecard.
